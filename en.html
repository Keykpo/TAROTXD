Tarot Reading FAQs

What should I pay attention to before a tarot reading?
Before starting an online tarot reading, we recommend finding a quiet environment and maintaining a peaceful state of mind without being disturbed by others or external influences. You can also choose the light music on our website to help you relax and meditate while selecting cards to establish a connection. Clarify the question you want to consult, and when drawing cards, silently recite or softly speak the question you want to ask in your heart, maintaining an open and objective attitude to accept the guidance of the tarot cards. Please remember that AI tarot is a tool for self-exploration and reflection, and the final decision is in your own hands.
Can I repeat readings for the same question?
It is not recommended to frequently perform readings on the same question in a short period of time, unless your situation has changed through your own efforts or the information from the previous reading can no longer provide much help. Otherwise, it is not recommended to repeatedly read the same question. The purpose of divination is to guide the current direction. Each reading represents the current energy state. Being too frequent will lead to results that are not objective enough, and multiple readings will also lead to confusion in energy. If the results are different multiple times, which one should you believe? This will confuse the answer. It is recommended to perform AI tarot reading again when there is an obvious change in the situation to give yourself new guidance and direction.
Can tarot cards predict the future? How to view tarot results rationally?
Divination is more about reflecting the current energy state and possible future development trends, rather than completely deterministic future predictions or absolute destiny, because the future will change due to people's behaviors and thoughts. However, tarot cards will provide you with the guidance and insights you need at the moment, helping you better understand the current situation and potential opportunities. But the future is still more in your own choices and actions. If the results are not as expected, don't think there is no way to resolve it. Think about how to avoid some situations from happening. The role of tarot is to prepare for a rainy day.
What are the taboos or limitations of tarot reading?
For moral and legal considerations, our AI tarot divination system will not answer sensitive questions about life safety, major disease diagnosis, legal disputes, or financial investments. For such questions, please be sure to consult relevant professionals. Online tarot reading is mainly used for personal growth, self-awareness, spiritual exploration, etc., and does not replace professional advice in medical and legal matters.
Are online tarot results accurate? How to correctly understand divination results?
The accuracy of AI tarot divination results depends on many factors. First is the quality of your question and whether it is clear enough. The following precautions will tell you how to ask better questions. At the same time, the accuracy of divination also depends on your trust in the diviner you choose and the cards drawn. If you are willing to believe in the current guidance of the universe, then this information is destined information and what the universe wants you to hear. So if you decide to read the cards, please maintain a sincere heart when asking questions. We also recommend treating divination results as a reference and reflection tool rather than absolute truth. You should also combine your specific situation to obtain valuable help. Remember that tarot is a tool to stimulate intuition and self-awareness, and the final right of interpretation and choice lies with you.
How to ask better questions?
Try not to ask very vague, empty, and worthless questions such as What am I doing? Here are some examples of quality questions, such as How will my future work fortune develop?I currently have two job offers, A and B, which should I choose?What does my current ambiguous partner think of me? The clearer and more specific the question, the better it will help the diviner and tarot cards connect with your energy and give you more direct and targeted advice and guidance.
